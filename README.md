## ðŸ“Œ Features

- **Add Expenses**: Quickly log personal expenses with category, description, and date.  
- **Budget Monitoring**: Set a monthly budget and get alerts if you exceed it.  
- **Overview Dashboard**: View total expenses and monthly summaries.  
- **Visualizations**:
  - Pie chart for category-wise spending
  - Line chart for daily expense trends  
- **Export CSV**: Download your expense data for offline analysis.  
- **Custom Categories**: Add, edit, or remove categories as per your needs.

## ðŸš€ How to Use

### Add Expenses
1. Go to the **"Add Expense"** tab.  
2. Choose a category, add description & amount, and select the date.  
3. Click **"Add Expense"**.

### View Overview
- Check your total and monthly expenses.  
- See budget alerts if you exceed your monthly limit.

### Visualize Spending
- Navigate to the **"Visualization"** tab for pie charts and line charts showing your spending trends.

### Settings
- Adjust your monthly budget and categories in the **"Settings"** tab.

### Export CSV
- Download all your expenses as a CSV from the **Overview** tab.
